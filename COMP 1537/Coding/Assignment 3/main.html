Customization Tips

There are some crucial things to remember when customizing your car. First of all if you want to do better you don't use the topper or antenna item slots. There is no other reason for this other than being a sweat. After this we want to make sure to only use the darkest versions of each colour. Along with this we want to use the most minimal, non animated decal that we own. This way your car actually rotates faster and is easier to control. Allowing you to unlock your full potential of being a sweaty player.

How to Rank Up Quick

If you're looking to improve your ranked games, this is where to go. We have tips from macro gameplay all the way to the small intricacies of car control. We are here to help people of all ranks, so even if you're bronze, you can be sure to find something for you. The very first and simplest tip we have is to always practice. When searching for a game or waiting for your duo to return, always make sure to be in a free play session. This keeps you warmed up and stay consistent with hitting the ball in strange positions.
